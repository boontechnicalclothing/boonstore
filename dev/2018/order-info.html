Design

We prefer designs to be in .ai (Adobe Illustrator) format. If you have a design needing minor edits (e.g. Logo and number placement, font change) we will gladly do it for free for a limited number of edits. You can let us know in your order email.

If you have designs in formats other than .ai (such as .psd or .pdf), you can still send them and we'll let you know if we run into image quality problems. Our team will take a look at your design files and will give you feedback.

Design services

We also offer jersey design services. For a fee of $110, we will create 3 original case designs for you to choose from.

Shipping and Delivery

Turnaround time is 4 weeks but best to inform us when exactly you will need the jerseys.

Payment

We require 40% downpayment to commence production, 60% (plus shipping if order is less than 40 items) upon receiving the delivered items.

We charge an additional 5% if payment is made through PayPal (for finance charges).

All other fees after shipment (i.e customs tax, value added tax) shall be shouldered by your team.

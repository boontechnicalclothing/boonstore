Order FAQ

Design

We have a required template for jerseys in .ai format. If you already have designs and need minor edits (e.g. Logo and number placement, font change) we can do it for free, just let us know.

If you have your designs in .psd or other image formats, you can also send them but image quality may be compromised. Our team can take a look at your files and will give you feedbacks.

We also offer jersey design services. For a fee of $110, we will create 3 original case designs for you to choose from.

Shipping

Shipping is FREE for orders of 40 items and more (combination of items i.e 20 long sleeves + 20 shorts is ok).

Turnaround time is 2 weeks but best to inform us when exactly you will need the jerseys.

Payment

We require 40% downpayment to commence production, 60% (plus shipping if order is less than 40 items) upon receiving the delivered items.

We charge an additional 5% if payment is made through PayPal (for finance charges).

All other fees after shipment (i.e customs tax, value added tax) shall be shouldered by your team.
